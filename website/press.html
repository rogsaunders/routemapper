About RouteMapper

RouteMapper is a professional field-mapping tool built for competitive rally organisers, outback fundraising events, tour operators, and adventurous explorers. Using voice notes, rally-standard icons, GPS automation, and instant exports to Rally Navigator, Hema Maps, Google Earth, and Guru Maps — it eliminates notebooks, lost data, and guesswork.

Boilerplate (copy/paste for articles)

RouteMapper is an Australian-built rally reconnaissance tool that captures GPS tracks, rally-standard icons, timestamps, and voice notes — and exports directly to Rally Navigator, Hema Maps, Google Earth, and Guru Maps. It is designed for rally organisers, outback 4WD tours, and anyone mapping remote routes where pen-and-paper can’t be trusted.
