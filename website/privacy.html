Privacy Policy

RouteMapper respects your privacy. We collect only the information needed to provide the RouteMapper service and to communicate with supporters and customers.

Information We Collect

  • Email addresses submitted via our Kickstarter notification form
  • App crash reports (anonymised)
  • Non-identifying usage analytics to improve features

What We Do Not Collect

  • No location data is sent to our servers unless you export a route yourself
  • No advertising tracking
  • No data sold or shared with third parties

How We Use Collected Data

Solely to notify users of updates, new releases, or Kickstarter campaign announcements.

Contact
